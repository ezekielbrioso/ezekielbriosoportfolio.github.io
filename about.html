ABOUT ME

I'm Ezekiel Brioso, 18 years old, based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. My world revolves around designing multimedia, and I am dedicated to constantly learning and evolving in this dynamic field. I have a passion for screenwriting, storytelling, photography, video editing, and graphic design.

My goal is to skillfully curate each of these artistic disciplines to craft visually appealing content that evokes meaningful connections and resonates with audiences. I believe in pushing boundaries and experimenting with different techniques to create compelling narratives and impactful designs. Through my work, I aim to blend creativity with functionality, striving to produce content that not only looks great but also communicates effectively and leaves a lasting impression.
